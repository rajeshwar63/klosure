# Refund &amp; Cancellation Policy

**Effective date:** May 4, 2026
**Last updated:** May 4, 2026

This Refund &amp; Cancellation Policy explains how subscription billing, cancellations, and refunds work on Klosure.ai (the "Service"). It is published as a standalone page so payment processors and customers can locate the terms quickly. The substance is also captured in our [Terms of Service](/terms).

Klosure is operated by **Rajeshwar Yelne**, a sole proprietor based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. For any billing question, write to **support@klosure.com** with the subject line "Billing".

---

## 1. Free trial

New accounts get a **14-day free trial** of the full Klosure experience. No payment information is required to start the trial.

- The trial begins the day you sign up and ends 14 calendar days later, at the end of that day in your local time zone.
- During the trial you can use every feature included in the **Closer** tier, including the Email + Notetaker integration where eligible.
- We will send a reminder email a few days before the trial ends.
- If you do not select a paid plan before the trial ends, your account becomes **read-only** — your data is preserved, but you cannot create or modify deals until you upgrade. See Section 6 for read-only data retention.

You are never charged anything during the trial.

- Prices shown **exclude applicable taxes**. GST is added at checkout for Indian customers; international customers are responsible for their own tax compliance.

You can see your current plan, seat count, next renewal date, and historical invoices on the [Manage subscription](/billing/manage) page in your account.

## 3. Adding seats (mid-cycle upsizing)

When you add a seat to your team mid-cycle:

- The new seat is **billed immediately**, prorated from today through the end of your current cycle. Razorpay calculates the proration on our behalf.
- Your monthly recurring charge increases on the next renewal to reflect the new seat count.
- The added seat is available in your team's seat pool the moment payment succeeds.

Example: you have 3 Closer seats (₹22,500/mo) renewing on the 18th. On the 8th you add a 4th seat. Razorpay charges roughly ₹2,500 today (₹7,500 × ~10/30 days) and your next renewal on the 18th becomes ₹30,000.

---

## 4. Removing seats (mid-cycle downsizing)

When you reduce your seat count mid-cycle:

- The reduction is **scheduled at the end of your current billing cycle** — your team keeps full capacity until the cycle you have already paid for ends.
- We do **not** issue a refund for unused seat-days in the current cycle. This is industry-standard SaaS practice and is consistent with our Terms of Service.
- From the next cycle onward, your monthly charge reflects the lower seat count.
- The "Manage seats" panel in /billing/manage shows the scheduled new seat count and the date it takes effect.

If you change your mind before the cycle ends, you can revert the scheduled reduction from the same panel or by emailing **support@klosure.com**.

## 5. Cancelling a subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

- Open your account → **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription**, then click **Cancel my subscription**.
- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You retain full access until that date.
- We do **not** charge a cancellation fee.
- We do **not** issue a pro-rata refund for the remainder of the current billing period after a self-service cancellation, except where required by law (see Section 7).
- After your access end date, your workspace becomes **read-only** for a 90-day data-retention window (see Section 6).

If you cannot reach the in-app cancel button (for example, you have lost access to the email on the account), email **support@klosure.com** from any verifiable channel — a previous billing email, your Razorpay payment receipt, or your registered phone number — and we will cancel the subscription manually.

---

## 6. After cancellation: data retention &amp; reactivation

For 90 days after your subscription ends, your account remains in a **read-only** state:

- Existing deals, chat history, meeting transcripts, and dashboards remain visible to authenticated team members.
- New deals, edits, AI coaching turns, and integrations are blocked.
- You can reactivate at any time during this window by selecting a paid plan; no data migration is necessary.

**90 days after the read-only date, the account and its associated data are permanently deleted**, except where law requires longer retention (for example, billing records under Indian tax law are retained for 8 years per the Privacy Policy).

We send two reminders before deletion (15 days and 5 days out) so you have multiple chances to preserve your workspace. After deletion, recovery is not possible.

## 7. Refund eligibility

Subscription fees are **non-refundable**, except as set out below.

We will issue a refund — usually as a credit reversed to the original payment method through Razorpay — in any of the following cases:

1. **Duplicate or erroneous charges.** If you were charged twice for the same cycle, or charged after a confirmed cancellation, contact **support@klosure.com** within **30 days** of the charge with your invoice number and we will refund in full.
2. **Material outage.** If the Service is materially unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours through no fault of yours, you may request a pro-rated service credit or refund for the affected days.
3. **First-charge errors during onboarding.** If your first charge was completed but the subscription failed to activate on our side (for example, a webhook miscarriage left the team in trial), we will either fix activation and let you keep the cycle, or refund the charge in full at your option.
4. **Where required by applicable law.** If the law in your jurisdiction grants you a mandatory refund right that overrides this policy (for example, certain consumer-rights statutes in the EU/UK), we will honour it.

**What is not refundable:**

- Unused portions of the current billing cycle after a self-service cancellation (Section 5).
- Seats that were added mid-cycle and then removed in the same cycle.
- Charges that were authorised by anyone with valid access to your account at the time of payment.
- Subscription periods more than 60 days old, unless covered by Section 7.4.

## 8. How to request a refund

Send an email to **support@klosure.com** with the subject line **"Refund request"** and include:

- The email address on the Klosure account
- The Razorpay payment ID (visible on the invoice email or in /billing/manage)
- The amount and date of the charge
- A short description of why you are requesting the refund

We respond to refund requests within **5 business days**. If the refund is approved, Razorpay returns the funds to the original payment method, typically within **5–10 business days** depending on your bank.

## 9. International customers (USD / AED)

Until Razorpay's international card processing is activated for our account, customers outside India have two paths:

- **Start the 14-day free trial right now** — no card needed. Use this to evaluate Klosure while we sort out international auto-debit.
- **Request a manual invoice** through the concierge form on /billing. We will send a Razorpay payment link in INR-equivalent within 24 hours; once paid, your team is activated for the cycle.

The same refund and cancellation rules described in this policy apply to manually-invoiced subscriptions.

## 10. Chargebacks &amp; disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute through your card issuer or bank, we ask that you first contact **support@klosure.com** so we can review the situation and process a refund directly where appropriate. Bank-side disputes carry administrative costs and can take weeks to resolve; a direct support conversation is almost always faster.

If a chargeback is filed, we may suspend the related subscription until the dispute is resolved.
